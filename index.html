About NewsSwing

NewsSwing is a personal news digest based which gathers catered news based on a reader's Facebook likes and political preferences. Catered news gradually changes bias over time, gently exposing readers to more and more divergent political views without them noticing - which prevents immediate dismissal of new content and makes readers more tolerant.
